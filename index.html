Our story
How we met

Lucca and Fiona have known each other since grammar school, though for years they were little more than familiar faces. It wasn't until junior year of high school that their paths finally crossed in a meaningful way. From that point on, the two were inseparable — it was immediately clear they brought the best out of each other.

Arizona & San Diego

Both ended up at Arizona State University, where their relationship continued to blossom. After graduation, they built a life together in San Diego — spending nearly four years soaking up the sun, the coast, and everything the city had to offer.

The proposal

On January 31st, 2026, Lucca proposed to Fiona at Sunset Cliffs — one of San Diego's most breathtaking spots. She said yes.

What's next

These days, Lucca and Fiona are back in San Francisco, closer to family and ready for the next chapter. On July 17th, 2027, surrounded by the people they love most, they'll make it official — and they eagerly anticipate everything that comes next.
