Our story
How we met

Lucca and Fiona have known each other since grammar school, though for years they were little more than familiar faces in the hallway. It wasn't until junior year of high school, in 2018, that their paths finally crossed, in a meaningful way. From that point of entanglement onward, the two were inseparable. Neither Lucca nor Fiona could imagine how much the other would materially change the trajectory of their lives. .

Arizona & San Diego

Both Lucca and Fiona ended up at Arizona State University, which, looking back, felt less like coincidence and more like the universe nudging them in the right direction. After both graduating college at ASU, they built a life together in San Diego, spending years soaking up the sun, the coast, and everything that city had to offer.

The proposal

On January 31st, 2026, Lucca proposed to Fiona at Sunset Cliffs in San Diego. She said yes.

What's next

Today, Lucca and Fiona are back in San Francisco, closer to family and ready for the next chapter. On July 17th, 2027, surrounded by the people they love most, they'll make it official. Both Lucca and Fiona genuinely cannot wait to get started and begin the next chapter of their life together.
